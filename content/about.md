There are thousands of YouTube videos where people watch movies and TV series for the first time.
They'll sit down in front of a TV and position their camera to face them in order to capture their
reaction, their emotions and their feedback. The edited, uploaded videos generally show snippets of
the movie with their face off to one side.

We love these "first time watching" (FTW) videos! Not only do they allow us to re-watch snippets
from our favourite movies, but we get to see other people's reactions as they experience them for
the very first time. Whether they laugh when the comic relief cracks a joke, shed a tear when the
lovers part ways, scream when the knife-wielding psycho bursts from the closet, or cheer when the
underdogs win the championship, their emotions are a big part of the appeal of these videos. Often
we'll finish watching one person react to a movie, then we'll want to see how other people react to
the same film.

This website was built to make it easy to do just that - search for your favourite movie or TV
series and see a list of all the FTW reactions in one place. This site has a list of YouTube
[channels](/channels) that produce FTW videos. It gets all the videos from each channel and matches
them with the [movies](/movies) and [TV series](/tv-series) they're reacting to (using
[TMDB](https://www.themoviedb.org/)).

Since this is largely a manual process, there are very likely to be mistakes. If you notice that a
video doesn't match the movie/TV series it's linked to, or the season/episode numbers are incorrect,
or we just haven't added your favourite FTW YouTube channel,
[let us know](https://github.com/NormPlum/FTW-Website/issues)!
